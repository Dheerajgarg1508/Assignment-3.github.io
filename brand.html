ABOUT US

Lindt & Sprüngli is recognized as a leader in the premium quality chocolate market, offering a wide range of products in over 120 countries worldwide. Throughout nearly 175 years of Lindt & Sprüngli's existence, it has become known as one of the most innovative and creative companies producing premium chocolate.

The beginnings of the Lindt & Sprüngli company were in 1845, when father and son first made solid chocolate in their small confectionery, Sprüngli & Son. Since then, the company has gone through many different eras, but the entrepreneurial spirit and passion for making chocolate have guided both the Sprüngli family and Rodolphe Lindt, who invented the first truly melted chocolate and later sold his business to Sprüngli, and are still alive today.

Today, Lindt & Sprüngli's quality chocolate products are manufactured in 12 of their own factories in Europe and the USA. These are distributed by 25 subsidiary companies and branches, in over 410 of their own stores, and also through a comprehensive network of over 100 independent distributors worldwide. With over 14,000 employees, the Lindt & Sprüngli Group reported sales of 4.1 billion CHF in 2017.
